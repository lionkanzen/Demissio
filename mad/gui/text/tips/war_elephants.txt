WAR ELEPHANTS
- A powerful unit available to: Mauryans, Ptolemies, Seleucids, Macedonians, Epirotes, Persians, and Carthaginians.

- "Fear" aura reduces the strength of nearby enemy soldiers and a "Trample" aura reduces the health of nearby enemy units.

- Vulnerable to javelin infantry and sword infantry to a lesser extent. Flaming pigs can be used to frighten elephants and reduce their effectiveness by -25%.
